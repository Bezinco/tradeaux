📖 Our Story
TradeAux is a coordination layer for global trade. Global trade moves trillions of dollars in goods every year, connecting producers, manufacturers, distributors, and buyers across every continent. Despite its scale and sophistication, much of the coordination behind international trade remains fragmented. Businesses often search for suppliers independently, manage trade information across multiple systems, communicate through countless emails and spreadsheets, and make shipping decisions with only a partial view of the wider market. Valuable opportunities for collaboration, consolidation, and efficiency can remain hidden simply because the right information never comes together at the right time.
The effects of this fragmentation can be seen throughout the supply chain. Research has shown that many freight containers operate with significant unused capacity, with studies reporting average utilization of around 65 percent. While many logistics solutions focus on improving how goods are packed once a shipment exists, the broader challenge often begins much earlier—before shipments are formed, when buyers, sellers, and logistics decisions are still developing. TradeAux was created to address that earlier stage of international trade.
We believe global trade becomes stronger when information is easier to discover, markets are better connected, and businesses have greater visibility into the opportunities around them. By bringing together trade intelligence, market participation, and AI-assisted coordination within a single platform, TradeAux aims to reduce unnecessary complexity while supporting more informed and efficient international commerce. Global trade does not need to be reinvented. It needs to be better connected. TradeAux exists to help make that possible.
