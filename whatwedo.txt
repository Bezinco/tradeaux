WHAT IS TRADEAUX?

TradeAux is a real-time trade clearing network for agricultural and industrial commodities where buyers, sellers, logistics providers, insurers, and financiers interact through coordinated automated bidding systems.

Unlike a traditional auction or exchange, TradeAux does not simply match a single buyer to a seller. Instead, it clears entire trade scenarios across multiple layers at once: product supply, pricing, logistics, payment terms, and financing.

Co-operatives and producers list aggregated inventory (such as raw coconuts or value-added derivatives), while buyer agents compete based on price ceilings, quantity needs, and contract preferences. At the same time, freight providers price logistics, insurers price risk coverage, and financial institutions bid on trade finance opportunities.

The system is driven by scenario-based optimization from seller intelligence (“Duhe”), which can restructure inventory outcomes—for example, suggesting that adding a small additional quantity unlocks a higher clearing price and allows full stock liquidation.

TradeAux continuously evaluates all inputs and clears trades based on total net value across the entire stack, not just the highest bid. This allows partial fills, aggregated lots, and dynamic matching across global participants.

The result is a continuously running, multi-layer marketplace that converts fragmented commodity supply chains into a coordinated, liquid global clearing system.
