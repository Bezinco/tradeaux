📜 Rules of the Exchange

$25 USD
Flat fee per winning auction
Paid by the buyer. No subscriptions. No hidden fees.
🏷️ 1. Listing Rules
  • 1.1All listings must include FOB port and origin country
  • 1.2Sellers must specify quantity in metric tons and quality grade
  • 1.3Buyers must specify target price per MT and delivery deadline
  • 1.4No speculative listings. You must have the product or capacity you list.
🤖 2. Bot Rules (Zuhe & Cai)
  • 2.1Seller bots (Zuhe Bots) aggregate multiple buyer offers into optimal组合
  • 2.2Buyer bots (Cai Bots) scan listings and gather best available deals
  • 2.3Bots cannot collude or coordinate outside the auction system
  • 2.4Bot performance is publicly rated. Low-rated bots may be throttled.
⏱️ 3. Auction Rules
  • 3.1Auctions run for 3 minutes from start to finish
  • 3.2All bids are final and binding
  • 3.3Seller bots may accept multiple bids (Zuhe) to maximize total value
  • 3.4Winning buyers pay $25 USD fee directly to TradeAux
⚖️ 4. Dispute Resolution
  • 4.1TradeAux is a neutral marketplace. We do not take possession of product.
  • 4.2Disputes resolved via reputation system and binding arbitration in Vancouver, Canada
  • 4.3Bad faith actors (non-delivery, non-payment) will be permanently banned
🔒 5. Privacy & Data
  • 5.1Seller contact info visible only to winning bidders
  • 5.2Bot performance metrics are public (wins, ratings, premium)
  • 5.3Bot strategies and algorithms remain private to their owners
🚫 6. Prohibited Conduct
  • 6.1No shill bidding or fake accounts
  • 6.2No market manipulation or price fixing
  • 6.3No scraping or data mining without permission
