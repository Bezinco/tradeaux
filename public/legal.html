2. Definitions

  • "Buyer" – A user who places bids on commodity or transport auctions.
  • "Seller" – A user who lists commodities for auction.
  • "Carrier/Broker" – A user who bids on transport contracts.
  • "Auction" – A time-limited bidding event for commodities or transport services.
  • "Brief" – A buyer's request for specific commodities or transport services.
  • "Bot" – An automated bidding tool configured by users.
  • "Winning Bid" – The highest valid bid at auction close (or lowest in transport auctions).
  • "Flat Fee" – The US $25.00 fee charged per auction or brief submission.

4. Auction Rules & Fee Structure

4.1 Flat Fee Model
TradeAux charges a flat fee of US $25.00 per auction (commodity or transport) and per brief submission. This fee is non-refundable except as expressly provided in Section 11.

4.2 Auction Types

  • Commodity Auction: Sellers list products; Buyers bid. Highest bid at closing wins.
  • Transport Auction: Carriers/Brokers bid on freight contracts. Lowest bid at closing wins.

4.3 Binding Nature of Bids
All bids placed on the Platform are irrevocable and binding once submitted. Automated bot bids are considered valid bids placed by the account owner.

4.4 Winner Obligations
The winning bidder must complete the transaction within the specified timeframe. Failure to do so may result in account suspension and negative reputation flags ("Note of Shame").

5. Buyer Obligations

Buyers agree to:

  • Maintain sufficient funds in their TradeAux wallet to cover auction fees and winning bids.
  • Complete payment to the Seller within 48 hours of winning a commodity auction.
  • Launch a transport auction or arrange shipping within 72 hours of winning a commodity auction.
  • Not engage in shill bidding, bid manipulation, or any fraudulent activity.
  • Provide accurate delivery information and destination details.

6. Seller Obligations

Sellers agree to:

  • Provide accurate and truthful descriptions of listed commodities (product, variety, grade, quantity, origin).
  • Honor winning bids and complete shipment as described.
  • Not cancel auctions after bidding has commenced without legitimate cause.
  • Provide necessary export documentation and comply with all applicable export laws.
  • Respond to buyer communications within 24 hours.

7. Carrier/Broker Obligations

Carriers and Brokers agree to:

  • Provide accurate pricing and transit time estimates.
  • Honor winning transport bids and execute the agreed-upon shipment.
  • Provide the $50 discount off invoice as specified in the transport auction terms.
  • Maintain valid licenses, insurance, and certifications required for the routes they bid on.
  • Notify all parties immediately of any delays or issues.

9. Payment & Wallet System

9.1 Wallet Functionality
All users maintain a virtual wallet for deposits, fee payments, and transaction settlements. Funds are held in accordance with Canadian financial regulations.

9.2 Deposits
Minimum deposit: $100 USD. Deposits are processed through third-party payment providers (e.g., Wise, Stripe). TradeAux does not store full payment card information.

9.3 Withdrawals
Users may withdraw wallet balances subject to verification and applicable processing fees. Withdrawal requests are processed within 5-7 business days.

9.4 Fee Collection
The $25 flat fee is deducted from the winner's wallet upon auction completion. For brief submissions, the fee is deducted at time of filing.

10. Transport & Delivery

10.1 FOB Terms
Unless otherwise specified, commodity auctions are offered on FOB (Free On Board) terms at the origin port. The Buyer is responsible for arranging and paying for transport from the origin.

10.2 Transport Auction Requirement
Buyers who win commodity auctions must either (a) launch a transport auction on TradeAux, or (b) provide proof of independently arranged shipping. Failure to do so may result in penalties.

10.3 Risk of Loss
Risk of loss transfers from Seller to Buyer upon loading at the FOB origin. TradeAux is not a carrier and assumes no liability for shipment delays, damages, or losses.

10.4 Carrier Discount
The winning Carrier/Broker must provide the advertised $50 discount off the final invoice, applied before payment.
