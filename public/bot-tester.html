🧮 DUHE DECISION LOGIC
How Duhe scores each bid
Price Score: 1 - (price / maxPrice)
Fit Score: 1 if fills exact gap, else 0.5
Payment Score: (mult - 1.0) × 5
EXW Score: savings / 100
Total = (price × 0.40) + (fit × 0.25) + (payment × 0.20) + (exw × 0.15)
