📖 Our Story
TradeAux was born from a simple observation: food surplus shouldn't go to waste while buyers pay premium prices. A coconut farmer in Fiji shouldn't struggle to sell their harvest while a distributor in Rotterdam pays inflated rates.
We built TradeAux to democratize global commodity trading. Our platform connects producers directly with buyers — eliminating layers of middlemen, hidden markups, and opaque pricing. Every auction is transparent, every fee is flat ($25), and every participant is held accountable by our public reputation system.
What started as an idea to help small co-ops access global markets has grown into a full ecosystem: commodity auctions, transport bidding, bot-powered trading, and real-time market intelligence. All with one mission — turning surplus into opportunity, one auction at a time.
