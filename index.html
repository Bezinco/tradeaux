What Is a Kontor?

A kontor is a connected trading hub where buyers, sellers, logistics activity and financial factoring are concentrated. TradeAux builds markets by focusing activity into kontors—so demand, supply, and freight can interact in one structured environment instead of being scattered. Each kontor grows as real trade activity increases..
