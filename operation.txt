DUHE BUYER EXPANSION & REVENUE MODEL

Within TradeAux, “Duhe” functions as the seller-side intelligence layer that actively optimizes inventory clearance and market outcomes. In addition to restructuring lots and improving pricing scenarios, Duhe can also identify and introduce additional qualified buyers into an active trade environment.

When Duhe successfully adds a new buyer to a live or pre-qualified auction scenario, that buyer becomes part of the bidding pool and increases competition, liquidity, and clearing efficiency. This directly improves outcomes for sellers by increasing demand density and improving price discovery.

Each successfully introduced buyer represents a monetized network expansion event within the system. A fixed fee of $75 is applied per added buyer, reflecting the value of verified demand injection into the marketplace.

This model aligns incentives across the platform: sellers benefit from deeper liquidity, buyers benefit from access to structured supply they would not otherwise reach, and TradeAux generates revenue through incremental network participation rather than transaction extraction alone.

Over time, Duhe evolves from a pricing and aggregation assistant into a demand-shaping engine that actively grows and optimizes both sides of the market, increasing efficiency and throughput across the entire TradeAux ecosystem.
