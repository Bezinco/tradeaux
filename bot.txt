Strategy 1: The Price Warrior (Conservative)
Setting	Value
Goal	Win at lowest possible price
Base CPM	$4.00
Floor CPM	$2.00
Step size	$0.05 (slow drops)
Comps	None (price only)
Aggression	Low
When to use: Creator has low costs, wants volume over margin.

Bot behavior: Drops price slowly. Never adds comps. Accepts losing if price goes too low.

Strategy 2: The Comp Master (Value-Added)
Setting	Value
Goal	Win by adding value, not cutting price
Base CPM	$4.50 (premium)
Floor CPM	$3.50 (high floor)
Step size	$0.10
Comps	High-res, pinned comment, end card, community post
Aggression	Medium
When to use: Creator has engaged audience, wants to preserve CPM.

Bot behavior: Keeps price high. Adds comps aggressively to climb rankings. Only drops price when out of comps.

Strategy 3: The Aggressor (Win At All Costs)
Setting	Value
Goal	Win, regardless of price or comps
Base CPM	$5.00
Floor CPM	$1.50 (very low)
Step size	$0.25 (fast drops)
Comps	All available
Aggression	High
When to use: Creator needs cash flow, building reputation, or testing new category.

Bot behavior: Drops price fast. Adds comps immediately. Will go to floor aggressively.

Strategy 4: The Opportunist (Smart Targeting)
Setting	Value
Goal	Only win when conditions are favorable
Base CPM	$4.00
Floor CPM	$3.00
Step size	$0.10
Comps	Conditional (only when needed)
Aggression	Variable (based on competition)
When to use: Creator wants profitable wins only, not just any win.

Bot behavior: Calculates estimated profit before bidding. Only enters if margin > 20%. Drops out if price gets too close to floor.

Strategy 5: The Hunter (Category Specialist)
Setting	Value
Goal	Dominate specific categories
Base CPM	$4.50 (Finance), $3.50 (Gaming), $5.00 (Beauty)
Floor CPM	Varies by category
Step size	$0.10
Comps	Category-specific (e.g., finance needs data share, gaming needs high-res)
Aggression	High for target categories, low for others
When to use: Creator has expertise in specific niches.

Bot behavior: Bids aggressively on chosen categories. Ignores or low-bids others. Adjusts comps based on category norms.

Strategy 6: The Arbitrageur (Buy Low, Sell High)
Setting	Value
Goal	Win auctions, then resell the views
Base CPM	$3.00
Floor CPM	$1.50
Step size	$0.05
Comps	Minimal (preserve margin)
Aggression	Low-Medium
When to use: Creator has access to cheap traffic they can resell.

Bot behavior: Bids very low. Only wins when competition is weak. Focuses on volume over margin per auction.

Strategy 7: The Hybrid (Adaptive)
Setting	Value
Goal	Adapt strategy based on real-time conditions
Base CPM	Dynamic
Floor CPM	Dynamic
Step size	Variable
Comps	AI-decided
Aggression	Adaptive
When to use: Creator wants hands-off, AI-optimized bidding.

Bot behavior: Learns from past auctions. Adjusts strategy based on win rate, competition, and profit margin. Self-optimizes over time.
